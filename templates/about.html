📱 About Weather Prediction

Weather Prediction is a web app that gives you real-time weather conditions and a 5-day forecast for any city in the world. Type a city name and instantly get temperature, humidity, wind speed, sunrise/sunset times, and what to expect over the coming days.

Live data is pulled from the OpenWeatherMap API, processed on the backend, and delivered cleanly to your browser — no page reloads, no clutter.

🐍 Flask ☁️ OpenWeatherMap API ⚡ Vanilla JS 🎨 CSS3 🐍 Python 3
